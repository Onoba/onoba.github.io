Tree Planting Inniative

As members of the Civil Society Organisations (CSOs) in Greater Nebbi, we wish to actively support the processes to bridge the development processes as mandated by law.  One critical area which has attractmnn+ed our attention is the high rate of environmental degradation in our Sub-region as a result of the high demand for Charcoal and Fuel wood as energy sources for cooking.  

Our implementation methodology to ensure sustainability is to establish woodlots at household level, Educational and Church Institutions (over 1200 in number), with technical support from the District Forestry Services.  We are going to receive about 11Million seedling from National Forestry Authority (NFA) secured funding from the Government of Uganda and Roofings Uganda Limited to promote tree growing in our communities.  

Interested people are advised to complete this form after which they are selected to participate in the programme.
